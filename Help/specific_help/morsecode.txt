**Description**
Encrypts a sentence to morse code or decrypts it to English.

**Aliases**
`{prefix}morse`, `{prefix}morsecode`

**Usage**
`{prefix}morsecode <encrypt/decrypt> <sentence>`
E.G.: `{prefix}morsecode ".... . .-.. .-.. --- --..-- / - .... .. ... / .. ... / -- --- .-. ... . / -.-. --- -.. ." decrypt`
